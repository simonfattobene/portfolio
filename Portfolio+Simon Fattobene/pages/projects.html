This logo is from and ongoing dietary supplements project, the idea wa to give the impresion of power and liberty, that's why I decided to evoque a grunge style. But at the same time I tryed to make it graceful with some effortless brush strokes.

From this point on I gave to the whole branding the same caracteristic that makes this style fresh and not totally defined.

You can acces the project instagram feed clicking it's name BBIG Supplements, here you will see how the logo style matches the project as a whole.

I made this one for a friend, he was starting an online delivery of healthy food. His main concerns for this project was to show to the audience that his food was fresh and healthy but he wanted them to notice that it was a delivery.

I started with the idea of a tire in motion to bring the impresion of movement to the table, and used vibrantly coloured foods, to enfatize the freshness of the product.

Nontheless one of the biggest things for him was to make it artistic, if you pay close attention it resembles a painting palette.
